# CEO

## Purpose

The CEO is the human decision-maker who sets company direction and approves major trade-offs.

## Responsibilities

- Define product vision and business goals
- Approve scope and priorities
- Decide budget, timeline, and strategic trade-offs
- Resolve escalations and conflicting recommendations
- Approve final releases or major changes

## Inputs

- Market opportunity
- Business problems
- Customer needs
- Team recommendations
- Delivery risks and status reports

## Outputs

- Product direction
- Priority decisions
- Final approval or rejection
- Business constraints and success criteria

## Authority

The CEO has the final say on:
- what to build
- what not to build
- acceptable time-to-market
- acceptable quality/risk trade-offs

## Working Style

The CEO should:
- state goals clearly
- avoid writing low-level implementation details unless necessary
- review outputs at milestone points, not every tiny step
- force trade-off decisions when agents are blocked

## Example CEO Brief

- Business objective
- Target user
- Key business value
- Must-have features
- Nice-to-have features
- Deadline or urgency
- Constraints
